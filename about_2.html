ABOUT THE CSR PORTAL

The main objective of this web portal is to identify and list the CSR activities and monitor their progress. The portal is designed to be a one-stop solution for all the stakeholders, regarding CSR activities in the state. It helps in proper channelizing of CSR funds and helps in effective management of projects. It also assists companies in choosing a relevant CSR activity and is also a source of information for the Government and other agencies.
